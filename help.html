Instructions

  1. Click on two cards to flip them.
  2. If they match, you have found a pair.
  3. If they don't match, they will unflip and you will have to remember their location.
  4. Everytime you click on a card, your score will go up by 1, so a lower score is better.
  5. Repeat steps 1-4 until all pairs have been found.
  6. Press the reset button if you want to play again.
